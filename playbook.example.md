## L001: Avoid entering on flat RSI

**Rule:** When RSI is between 45–55 (neutral zone), skip entries even if BB
touch and trend agree. Neutral RSI means indecision — wait for conviction.

**Created:** 2025-01-01

## L002: Respect strong news contrary to technicals

**Rule:** When news sentiment is strongly bearish (< -0.25) and the only
signal is a technical long entry, skip the trade. News-driven moves can
overwhelm technical patterns in the short term.

**Created:** 2025-01-01
**Evidence:** none (seed lesson)

## L003: Reduce size after consecutive losses

**Rule:** After 3+ consecutive losses, use size_multiplier ≤ 0.3 for the
next trade. Consecutive losses may indicate a regime change — reduce
exposure until the pattern clarifies.

**Created:** 2025-01-01
**Evidence:** none (seed lesson)
